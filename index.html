About the Event

The Ideathon 2025 The School of Engineering and Technology, Department of Information Technology, in association with TechTitans, proudly presents IDEATHON 2025 — a platform designed to ignite innovation, creativity, and problem-solving skills among students.

Students will submit their idea abstracts on 03.11.2025, which will be evaluated by the organizing committee. The shortlisted participants will be announced on 04.11.2025, and the final presentation will take place on 06.11.2025 at the 3rd Floor Lecture Hall, Academic Block.

Participants can choose from diverse problem domains such as Technology, Environment, Healthcare, Education, and Social Innovation, and propose creative, feasible, and impactful solutions.

Rules & Guidelines

To ensure a fair and exciting event for everyone, please adhere to the following rules:

  • Team Size: Each team can have a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 2 members.
  • Eligibility: All participants must be currently enrolled as students in any college or university.
  • Originality: All ideas and submissions must be the original work of the team. Plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Problem Statement: Teams must select one of the 20 provided problem statements as the foundation for their idea.
  • Abstract: A clear and concise abstract (max 500 words) describing your proposed solution must be submitted via the registration form.
  • Code of Conduct: All participants must treat each other with respect. Harassment or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Submission: Students will submit their idea abstracts on 03.11.2025, which will be evaluated by the organizing committee. The shortlisted participants will be announced on 04.11.2025, and the final presentation will take place on 06.11.2025 at the 3rd Floor Lecture Hall, Academic Block.
  • Judging: The decision of the judging panel is final and binding. Criteria will include innovation, feasibility, impact, and presentation quality.

Problem Statements

Choose one of these challenges to focus your innovation.

PS-01: Technology

Smarter Digital Security Against Online Scams

Cyber fraud and digital scams are increasing every year. Design an AI-driven security system to protect users from online financial threats and scams.

PS-02: Technology

Digitization of Public Records

Many public institutions rely on outdated paper records. Build a secure and efficient digital solution to manage and protect government data.

PS-03: Technology

Inclusive Tech for Senior Citizens

Many elderly users struggle with digital platforms. Create accessible interfaces or tools that simplify technology for senior citizens.

PS-04: Technology

Authenticity in Online Recruitment

Fake job listings and recruitment scams are on the rise. Develop a verification platform for job seekers to identify genuine opportunities.

PS-05: Environment

Tracking and Reducing Carbon Footprints

Cities face severe air pollution. Build a system that helps citizens monitor their carbon footprint and take steps to reduce it.

PS-06: Environment

Smarter Rainwater Harvesting

Rural areas face water scarcity despite rainfall. Design a low-cost, tech-enabled system to improve local rainwater harvesting and distribution.

PS-07: Environment

Efficient Waste Segregation and Recycling

Improper waste segregation causes environmental harm. Propose a tech-based solution to improve household-level waste sorting and recycling.

PS-08: Environment

Sustainable EV Charging Infrastructure

EV adoption is growing, but charging points are limited. Create a sustainable, data-driven network for accessible EV charging stations.

PS-09: Healthcare

Lifestyle Disease Prevention

India faces rising lifestyle diseases like diabetes and obesity. Develop a digital health companion to promote daily awareness and prevention.

PS-10: Healthcare

Rural Emergency Healthcare Access

Rural regions lack timely medical care. Build an app or network to connect ambulances and telemedicine support for remote patients.

PS-11: Healthcare

Reliable Digital Health Information

Many users self-diagnose from unreliable content. Design a verified health info portal using AI or expert curation to ensure accuracy.

PS-12: Healthcare

Smart Blood Donation Network

Blood shortages are common. Develop a digital platform that connects donors and hospitals instantly during emergencies.

PS-13: Education

Affordable Digital Education for Rural Schools

Many rural students lack access to digital tools. Build a low-cost, offline-ready platform for interactive learning experiences.

PS-14: Education

Bridging Industry and Academia

Many graduates lack practical job skills. Design a collaboration platform connecting universities and industries for skill development.

PS-15: Education

Tackling Exam Stress and Anxiety

Students struggle with stress and performance anxiety. Propose a solution to support mental health within educational environments.

PS-16: Education

Reducing School Dropouts Using Data

Dropout rates remain high in low-income areas. Use data analytics to identify at-risk students early and prevent school dropouts.

PS-17: Social Innovation

Youth Employment & Skill Matching

Youth unemployment is a major issue. Build a platform that connects skills, training, and opportunities efficiently.

PS-18: Social Innovation

Empowering Rural Women Entrepreneurs

Rural women face barriers to entrepreneurship. Create an accessible digital ecosystem that supports their business growth.

PS-19: Social Innovation

Safer Roads through Smart Awareness

Road accidents due to speeding and poor awareness are common. Propose a tech-enabled solution to improve driving safety and traffic discipline.

PS-20: Social Innovation

Inclusive Infrastructure for All

People with disabilities face accessibility issues. Design inclusive public and digital infrastructure to ensure equal access for everyone.
